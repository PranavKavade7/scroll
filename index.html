What is Scroll

A home built for AI creators.
Finally.

Instagram wasn't built for AI content. YouTube doesn't understand it. Twitter is too chaotic. Scroll is the first platform designed from the ground up for videos made with AI — with tools, community, and a feed that actually gets it.

AI-only vertical feed

A TikTok-style feed — but every single video is made with AI. No real footage, no mixing. Pure AI content. You always know what you're watching, and you're always amazed by it.

Creator profiles & followers

Build your identity as an AI creator. Get followers based on your work. Show which AI tools you use. Earn a verified badge. Your profile is your portfolio — and your proof of skill.

"How I made this" layer

Every video has a tab showing the prompt, tool, and workflow the creator used. You don't just watch — you learn. This feature alone makes Scroll the most educational platform for AI video in the world.

Weekly challenges & prizes

Every week, a new creative challenge theme. The best AI video wins — cash prizes, featured placement, brand partnerships. This isn't passive viewing. It's a competitive creative community.
